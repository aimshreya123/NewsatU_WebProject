FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS



Q: When can I reach someone by phone in your Customer Care Department?
 A: Our Customer Care Department is open from 6AM to 5PM Monday to Friday, 6AM to 10AM Saturday and 6AM to 11AM on Sunday.


Q: What time are newspapers usually delivered?
A: Newspaper delivery is completed by 6:30am everyday. Please call us or email us if you need your delivery earlier.


Q: What am I able to do on my customer dashboard?
A: You have the ability to add several delivery addresses, move your account, pause and restart delivery, submit billing inquiries, add new newspaper and magazines to your order, enter a delivery concern, make a one time payment on your account and submit an inquiry to a News@U's Customer Care representative. If you have additional questions, please give us a call at 800-662-2275.


Q: What should I do if my paper is wet, damaged or missing?
A: You can submit your request for a redelivery on your customer dashboard, call us at 800-662-2275 or email us at deliveryconcern@gmail.com. A replacement copy will arrive within two hours if contacted by 10AM.


Q: How will I know if my delivery is delayed or actually missing?
A: Register your account on our website or give us a call and supply us with your email address. If for any reason we are experiencing major delays, you will be contacted via email.


Q: How do I get credit for a missed delivery?
A: You can submit your request on your customer dashboard, call us at 800-662-2275 or email us at deliveryconcern@newsatu.com


Q: How do I put a temporary suspension on my delivery?
A: You can submit your request on your customer dashboard, call us at 800-662-2275 or email us at suspension@newsatu.com


Q: Can you hold my newspapers/magazine while I am away and send them all when I return?
A: Yes, please contact our Customer Care department at 800-662-2275 or email us at suspension@newsatu.com and we will gladly arrange for this.


Q: I am moving, can News@U continue to deliver my newspapers and magazines?
A: Yes, on your customer dashboard you have the ability to enter your new delivery address and the date you would like to move as of. You can also contact our Customer Care department at 800-662-2275 or email us at moving@newsatu.com


Q: How can I get onilne access to a publication you already deliver to me?
A: Please contact our Customer Care department at 800-662-2275 or email us at info@newsatu.com and we will gladly help you get setup.


Q: Can I order a back issue of a publication?
A: Yes, please contact our Customer Care department at 800-662-2275 or email us at info@newsatu.com we will gladly check on the availability and pricing.


Q: Can I pay my bill online?
A: Yes, you can pay a one time bill online by logging in to your customer dashboard. We do not accept recurring payments online.


Q: How can I update my recurring payment details?
A: To update your credit card details, please contact our Customer Care department at 800-662-2275 or email us at billinginquiry@gmail.com


Q: My early morning carrier is exceptional and I would like to send him/her a tip for their hard work.
A: Please contact our Customer Care department if you wish to give your carrier a tip. You can reach us at 800-662-2275


Q: What publications does News@U offer for home and office delivery?
A: Please take a look at our newspaper and magazine catalogs on our website. If you can’t find something you are looking for please send us an email at info@newsatu.com and we will make all efforts to find what you are looking for. We love a good challenge!


Q: I would like to order some publications on an ongoing basis but I can not find the pricing?
A: At News@U we customize our service to meet our customer’s needs. Please add all of the publications you are interested in, into your shopping cart. Once you submit the shopping cart, one of our Customer Care representatives will contact you with a detailed proposal. We never start an account without following-up with you first.


Q: I would like to order a specific publication one time?
A: Please add the publication you are interested in, into your shopping cart. Once you submit the shopping cart, one of our Customer Care representatives will contact you. We never start an account or make a one time delivery without following-up with you first.


Q: I would like to gift a friend an annual subscription to a newspaper/magazine?
A: Please contact our Customer Care department. They will gladly get you setup with this very generous gift.
